Will I be the dumbest person?

As you might know, a huge amount of web developers is self-tought. Many of us studied in unconventional ways and luckily survived by winging it. At CAFE ROBOT we put a lot of focus on being inclusive for people with any level of experience.

Will I be the smartest person?

Hi there, modest fellow! We also very much appreciate more experienced web developers to join our event and help out with any questions. However we do not support spitting sick jargon-lines, so this is your chance to get into teaching beginners. All of us had to start somewhere, right?

I hate networking and would rather jump into a Canal in the Red Light District.

Yes, we've all been there. By calling this the 'first non-awkward Tech-Meetup', we commit to facilitating conversations and introducing you to people who could answer your questions. And most of them are also up for cracking 1-5 silly jokes to make you feel better.

What is your hidden agenda?

Great news: There is none. I decided to start this meetup to create an inclusive community for (future) web-developers in Amsterdam. I very much aim to keep this event-series small and personal, to encourage helpful conversations and the opportunity to make some new friends. At this point in time that also means that we don't have a sponsor, which is why you have to buy your drinks yourself. :)

WE ARE LOOKING FOR YOU

CAFE ROBOT Meet-ups are small and intimate with around 15 people per event. We are always on the look-out for cool locations to crash for around 2-3 hours on a weekday once a month.

Our dream locations are unexpected (think laundry shop, hair salon, empty swimming pool) and ideally for free. If you happen to know somebody with access to such a place, or have any other pointers, please get in touch with us!
