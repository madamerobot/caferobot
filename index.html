BE NOSEY

Will I be the dumbest person?

As you might know, a huge amount of web developers is self-tought. Nobody learnt anything the 'right way' and luckily survived by winging it. In this meet-up, we put a lot of focus on being inclusive for any level of experience.

Will I be the smartest person?

Will I be the smartest person? Hi there, modest fellow! We also very much appreciate more experienced web developers to join our event and help out with any questions. All of us had to start somewhere, right?

I hate networking and would rather jump in a Canal in the Red Light District.

Yes, we've all been there. By calling this the 'first non-awkward Tech-Meetup', we really try to facilitate conversations, introduce you to people who could be helpful for your questions and are willing to crack 1-5 silly jokes to make you feel better.

What is your hidden agenda?

Great news: There is none. I decided to start this meetup to create an inclusive community for (future) web-developers in Amsterdam. I very much aim to keep this event-series small and personal, to encourage helpful conversations and the opportunity to make some new friends.
